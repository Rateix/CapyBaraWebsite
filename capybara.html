Capybara Restaurant

About Us

Capybara Restaurant is a virtual dining experience that is part of the incredible VR game "Proboscis." Run by two masterminds, Rateix and Jimmybob, Capybara Restaurant takes players on a culinary adventure that is beyond compare. From the moment you enter this digital eatery, you are transported to a world where taste and texture come to life in the most extraordinary ways. The walls are adorned with vibrant and colorful murals that tell the story of the restaurant's origins and the incredible journey that led to its creation. The aroma of the freshly cooked food wafts through the air, enticing your senses and making your mouth water with anticipation.
